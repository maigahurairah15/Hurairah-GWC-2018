Leos

Kind and big-hearted. The Lions are always admired for their kindness and helpful nature. ... Energetic. Since they are ruled by Sun, the source of infinite energy, the Leo are energetic individuals, and spread the positive vibes around. Optimistic,Straightforward,Loyal.
Foods Leos Eat:
A fire sign, lavish Leos enjoy meals with rich, bold flavors and a little spice. No boring, bland dishes for you! Things that are slathered in curry or barbecue sauce are usually a hit with your palette. Food you might love: Korean barbecue, coconut curry, cinnamon rolls
